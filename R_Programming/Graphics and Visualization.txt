3. ggplot2
grammer of graphics, Lilly walkinson and Hadley wickman
By adding high level aspects of data visualizations 

ggplot2(
	data = df,
	aes(x = name, y = value)) +
geom_bar(
	stat = "identity",
	fill = alpha('#FF681D',0.15)) +
ggtitle("Hello World") +
xlab("Name") +
ylab("Value")
